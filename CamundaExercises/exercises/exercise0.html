Draw a Twitter QA Process Model

Goal

Discuss as a group a common process model to fulfill the needs of Anti Agile Inc.

Short description

Try to find the activities and events of the process to fulfill the needs of Anti Agile Inc. Model the process on a whiteboard or flipchart.

Detailed Steps

  1. Discuss the requirements of Anti Agile Inc.
  2. Model the Twitter QA process as a group on a whiteboard or flipchart.
  3. Discuss the process model(s) with the trainer afterwards.
